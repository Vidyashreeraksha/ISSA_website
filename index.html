WHAT IS ISSA?

Information Systems Security Association (ISSA) is a not-for-profit, international professional organization of information security professionals and practitioners. It was founded in 1984, after work on its establishment started in 1982.

ISSA promotes the sharing of information security management practices through educational forums, publications and networking opportunities among security professionals.

ISSA has an international membership base. The headquarters of ISSA is located in Vienna, Virginia.

NIE – ISSA STUDENT CHAPTER

In an initiative taken up by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering under the guidance of Dr. G Ravi(Principal, NIE), Dr. H. D. Phaneendra(HOD CSE) and Dr. Shrirang. A Kulkarni(Professor, CSE), Ambitious Fourth semester students of The National Institute of Engineering, Mysore have collaborated to form and manage a local ISSA chapter at the college for the benefit of the student community.

  • A vision to improve and enhance every student’s technological skills.
  • Professional development or educational programming offerings.
  • Various technical, vocational and recreational activities dealing with Computer Science, IT and especially in Information Security will be held
  • Creates many avenues for the students to demonstrate their expertise through hack-a-thons and various other contests and learn application based technology the practical way.
  • Every ISSA member will have a lot to look up to and gain from this local chapter which can not only help them expand their knowledge base but to help in career.
